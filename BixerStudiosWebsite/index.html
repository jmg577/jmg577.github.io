Hey,

I’m Abel. I was born and raised in Monrovia, Liberia and currently reside in the Philadelphia Area. I’m a Philly based photographer that takes pride in capturing special moments. Everything has a story, and it’s my passion to tell these stories with my camera! By capturing special moments, we make them live forever, and bringing life to images puts a smile on people’s faces that’s priceless.

Big plans to travel the world and pin it. Seeing, listening, and meeting with amazing people and creating something spectacular. A story that highlights cultures, and the society we live in.

Photography has always been a part of my life. At one time I did not have the equipment to share what my internal lens saw. But well now … I can! And I’m looking forward to an amazing journey and to all the amazing people I will encounter!

Also… I love black and chamomile tea!

~ Abel Naklen Jr.
